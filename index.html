About Comtellion Services

Comtellion Services is a trusted service provider located at the University of Port Harcourt (Uniport) campus. We specialize in assisting students, graduates, and professionals with essential services that simplify bureaucratic processes.

With years of experience and a deep understanding of the Nigerian educational and administrative systems, we provide reliable and efficient services for NYSC, NERD NIN, JAMB, Remita, and other essential documentation needs.

Our team is dedicated to ensuring that our clients receive prompt, professional service with attention to detail. We understand the importance of these documents and work diligently to ensure they are processed correctly and efficiently.

Our Leadership

Engr Charles Uwakwe

Chief Executive Officer

Engr Charles Uwakwe is the visionary founder and CEO of Comtellion Services. With over 5 years of experience in student services and administrative support, he has built Comtellion into a trusted name for essential services at the University of Port Harcourt and beyond.

His expertise spans across NYSC processing, NIN enrollment, JAMB services, and various other documentation needs. Engr Charles Uwakwe is passionate about simplifying bureaucratic processes for students and professionals, ensuring they can focus on their academic and career goals.

Under his leadership, Comtellion Services has helped thousands of students navigate complex administrative requirements with efficiency and professionalism.
